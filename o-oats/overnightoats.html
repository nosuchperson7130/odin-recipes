Overnight Oats

A high protein breakfast option that provides good amounts of fiber. It's quick and easy to make. Doesn't require many ingredients. And cost effective. It's also highly customizable. You can change up any of the ingredients to your liking.

Ingredients List

  • 2/3 cup of oats. Roller preferrably
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 scoop protein powder
  • 3/4 cup plain fat free greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 tbsp of peanut butter

Directions

  1. Add the oats, plain fat free greek yogurt, and protein powder into a container of your choice.
  2. Add in about a half cup of water and mix until everything is well combined.
  3. Place in the fridge overnight or for at least 8 hours to soften up the oats.
  4. Right before eating, top it off with a banana and peanut butter. You can choose any other toppings if you'd like.
