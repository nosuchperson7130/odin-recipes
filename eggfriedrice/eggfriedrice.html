Egg Fried Rice

This egg fried rice recipe is easy to cook and prep. It's high in protein and has a healthy amount of veggies. You only need a few ingredients and you can always customize what veggies go into it. It's a great option for those on the go or just don't have time to cook within the week.

Ingredients List

  • 3 Whole eggs
  • 1.5 cups of cooked white rice
  • 1/2 cup of frozen green peas
  • 2 carrots diced
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, soy sauce, for seasoning

Directions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat with non-stick spray
  2. Add eggs and scramble them in the pan
  3. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, soy sauce, and any other seasoning you prefer
  4. Put the eggs to the side once fully cooked
  5. Add the frozen peas and diced carrots to the pan
  6. Add your seasonings for the veggies
  7. Once the veggies are softened, add the rice
  8. Add soy sauce to taste and stir together
  9. Reintroduce the eggs into the pan to heat up the eggs
  10. Enjoy!
